How the Game Works

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, usually of five players each, oppose one another on a rectangular court, typically indoors. The objective is to shoot a basketball through the opponent's hoop to score points.

Rules

The basic rules of basketball include dribbling the ball, passing, shooting, and scoring. Fouls are also a part of the game, with specific rules on personal and technical fouls.

Skills

Key skills in basketball include shooting, dribbling, passing, and defense. Players must practice these skills consistently to improve their performance in games.

Legendary Players

Many players have left their mark on the game of basketball, including Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, and many others who have inspired countless fans and aspiring athletes.
